# LCA Universe Project Charter

## Mission

Build the Future Together.

## Vision

Create the world's most satisfying factory universe where every player can build something worth remembering and sharing.

## North Star

Players should be proud of what they create.

## Core Principles

- Beauty before complexity.
- Calm before excitement.
- Respect player time.
- Reward curiosity.
- Creation over consumption.
- Mobile-first, PC-enhanced.
- Every update adds something beautiful.
- Creative expression must not be locked behind payment.
- A still screenshot should remain beautiful.

## Definition of Success

A player says:

- "This is my factory."
- "Come visit my world."
- "I made this."
- "I want to share this."

## Product Direction

LCA begins as a factory progression game.

It then expands into:

1. Factory growth
2. Collection
3. Customization
4. Landscape creation
5. Social visiting
6. Blueprint sharing
7. Collaborative civilization building

The clicker is the onboarding layer.

Creation is the long-term destination.
