# LCA Universe Bible

## Philosophy

LCA is a universe where technology and beauty coexist.

Players do not simply consume content.

They create it.

---

## The Player

The player is not a warrior.

The player is:

- Engineer
- Builder
- Designer
- Researcher
- Creator

---

## The Core

Everything begins with the Core.

The Core is the source of:

- Energy
- Automation
- Technology
- Civilization

As the Core evolves, the world evolves.

---

## Emotional Journey

Every update should strengthen one or more of these feelings:

1. Curiosity
2. Satisfaction
3. Discovery
4. Pride
5. Creativity
6. Sharing

---

## Long-term Vision

LCA grows through three ages.

### Age I

Factory

Players build efficient factories.

---

### Age II

World

Factories become beautiful environments.

Players shape landscapes.

---

### Age III

Universe

Players connect worlds together.

Civilizations emerge.

---

## The Golden Rule

If a feature increases numbers but does not increase wonder,

do not build it.
